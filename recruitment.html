What We Provide

  1. Full-cycle academic development support: from joint Ph.D. training (with housing stipend) to undergraduate research practice (outstanding students can fast-track to Ph.D. programs), covering every key stage of academic growth.
  2. A positive and healthy working atmosphere: we work hard when it is time to work and enjoy life when it is time to relax. The group regularly provides activity funds for colorful team-building events to promote communication and collaboration among members.
  3. Personalized career path planning: whether you aim for further study or a direct career, we will do our best to support your needs.

 

Important Notes

  1. Interest & dedication first: recruitment is a mutual selection process. For research, genuine interest and dedication matter far more than degrees or certificates. If you aspire to excel in academia (professorship, R&D, tech innovation, etc.), I will fully support every step of your development—professional guidance, ample exchanges, and proactive recommendations. Conversely, if you only seek a diploma, please refrain from contacting me at this stage.
  2. Mentorship & character building: two relationships in life are irreversible—blood ties and mentor–mentee ties. Schools and supervisors often play decisive roles in one’s growth. My mentors gave me immense positive influence; as your advisor, I will lead by example and hope to maintain close, long-term contact (not disappearing after graduation). Publishing papers matters, but helping you acquire real skills, build strong peer networks, and ultimately find your own stage is even more meaningful.

 

How to Apply
